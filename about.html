About Me

Hi, I am Madeline Fisher, a soon to be graduate from the University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor of Science degree in Arts and Entertainment Technologies, Hook'em Horns! My background is in game design where I specialized in Narrative Design and made a few fun projects. Now I am a UI/UX Designer, pursuing a certificate in UI/UX Design from the University of Texas at Austin Bootcamp. My biggest inspiration for doing UI/UX is to be able to help people on a daily basis. I also love figuring out puzzles so having the challenge of finding real solutions to user frustrations is always a fun and very rewarding challenge to take on.
